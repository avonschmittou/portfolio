GitHub for Creatives

Problem: Coworkers needed a guide on getting started with GitHub.
Solution: Instructional video with focus on the part that was particularly confusing to people.
Result: Decreased time to get started by 40% and increased comfort level of users by 80%.
Tools Used: Synthesia AI video, AI to create initial script outline, AI avatar, Screenshots edited with Preview, Content library background music

Adding Text to an Image in Canva

Problem: Employees rely on one or two people to teach/reteach others on process.
Solution: Microlearning course for employees with step-by-step process that was easily added to LMS. Elements in training mimic the process to give learners practice before trying it on Canva.
Result: Decreased time by 75% that the team spent on repeating the lesson for new employees or as a refresher for existing employees.
Tools Used: Storyline, Screenshots, AI voice/captions, Camtasia Video

Logging Into Canva

Problem: Microlearning course needs video to show related process.
Solution: Screen recording with voiceover embedded into course.
Result: Decrease training for people who already know how to log in with an outside account, saving 15% in training time and costs.
Tools Used: Camtasia, Audiate, Storyline

Getting Unstuck

Problem: Lesson materials on setting and achieving goals.
Solution: ILT with training slides, trainer guide, and participant guide to use with each training session. Can be altered as required and accessed on-the-go. Provided Word, Canva, and PDF versions to client.
Result: Increase group morale and cooperation by 30%
Tools Used: Canva, AI summary and WCAG check, MS Word, PPT

Over for MTSS/RTI
